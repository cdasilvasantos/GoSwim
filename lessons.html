Lessons

Our basic swimming techniques are designed to familiarize beginners with the water and also teach them how to stay afloat without much effort. Afterward, beginners can turn their attention to a specific swimming stroke by practicing the series of drills for that swimming stroke. Experienced swimmers who want to improve their swimming technique may also want to practice the series of swimming drills specific to each swimming stroke.
