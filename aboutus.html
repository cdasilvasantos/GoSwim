About GoSwim

GoSwim was created in 2023, by Chiara daSilva Santos. As a swimmer herself, she felt the need to spread her experience and knowledge in the sport, to help those that would like to become better and safer swimmers.

GoSwim offers video tutorials, explaining in depth the many different techniques needed to be able to float, tread, and ultimately learn the four strokes of freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly. Dive in and have some fun, it's going to be an amazing experience from here on out!
